HERE'S OUR DEAL

Our Mission

Committed to helping people enjoy the sweet excesses of life. Sugar? Yes please! Carbs? Never too many! Fat? Gotta have a little of that. Treat yo' self!


Our Background

Just Eat It is a small, locally run shop owned by people who love dessert. We are in the business of bringing happiness to people without the guilt. Our motto is "Treat Yo' Self" and we certainly live by that every day. The more sugar, the more fun. So bring your friends and family by our shop. It's the perfect place to hang out or grab something quick to satisfy your craving. And, we now deliver! So you can Just Eat It wherever you are!


Our Vibe

We've been enjoying sweet excess since 2010. We're a fun loving bunch who wants to bring that to our customers. We're passionate about dessert and we want you to be too. Don't look at the labels because you won't find anything useful. Plus, who cares? Don't take yourself too seriously and live a little.
